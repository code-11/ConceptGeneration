-A Warhamer 40k fanfic.
-A Group of mercenaries and a team of inquisition members is aboard a ship
-They are given specific instructions to infiltrate a planet and disable its operations
-Along the way, they have to pass near the eye of terror
-They crash land on the planet only to discover that the inhabitants are seemingly not chaotic at all
-Quite the opposite, they lead normal peaceful lives.
-They try to figure out why this is and what is going on.
-Chaos is chaos, that means eventually it would produce something not chaotic
-The inquisitor says to do nothing; if they interfere they will be seen as the enemy
-The inquisitor counts on the tendency for chaos to destroy itself.
-Eventually a chaos craft lands on the planet and begins to massacre the occupants
-They steal it and leave the planet.
