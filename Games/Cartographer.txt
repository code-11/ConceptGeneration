Cartographer

-You play as an ex-prisoner
-as a loser of the recent power shift, you have been sent to the recently discovered frozen wastes
-You now must be a cartographer
-Primary mechanic is the ability to draw maps which you can use yourself or sell to others
-Rewarding Exploration 
-Map over world and ruins/dungeons
-Can't fight though, have to encourage adventurers to help you
-their sucess is dependent on your maps (somehow)
-Survival mechanics, food, water, tiredness, hot, cold
