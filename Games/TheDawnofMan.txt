The Dawn of Man
-Text based community RPG ish thing
-Like an RTS or TBS but text based
-You control and early stone/bronze age society as it matures
-Economy
-Military/Defense/wild animals
-Food ie farming, fishing hunting
-Religion
-Disease
-Other tribes
-Politics
-Some other worldly elememts like comets and radiation
-Events happen and you have to respond
	-A villager has gotten wounded, help or not
	-A near by village is under attack, help or not?
-Like D&D, detail generates as needed.
